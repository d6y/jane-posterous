title: Lightroom for Digital Photographers - RAW images date: 2008/05/02 00:32:00 -0700 tags: [{"name":"photography","id":3774167}] author: Jane Dallaway alias: /lightroom-for-digital-photographers-raw-image The Lightroom for Digital Photographers videocast covered an interesting and illustrative topic this week What's the benefit of RAW images?.
