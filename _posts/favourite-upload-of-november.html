Skitters at sunrise on a low tide Brighton beach
Originally uploaded by Jane Dallaway
In November I uploaded 2991 photos, but a lot of those were because we migrated all the photos we had previously hosted over at janeandrichard

This photo is of Skitters, taken on a low tide early morning walk in Hove as the sun rose.

This photo was taken with my dog-walking camera, the Pentax PC-33 loaded with Kodak Portra 400VC.

I have chosen this photo mainly because I love the subdued and calming colours. The photo has had no post processing. The film was processed and scanned by Colourstream in Brighton.
