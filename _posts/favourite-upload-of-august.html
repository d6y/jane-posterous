Bandstand
Originally uploaded by Jane Dallaway
In August I uploaded 117 public photos to flickr.

This photo is of the newly renovated bandstand in Brighton and was taken with a point and shoot film camera (my Pentax PC-33) loaded with my current favourite film, Kodak Portra VC400, whilst out walking the dog one morning.

The photo has had no post processing. The film was processed and scanned by Colourstream in Brighton.
