A couple of weeks ago Simon ran a "Top tips for communication" session in the Madgex offices. One of the books he referred to was The Jelly Effect by Andy Bounds (which I have downloaded the sample for the kindle app on my iPhone - I love the sample chapter thing, a great way to determine if it is a book I'm actually likely to read). The other day whilst browsing the internet for more information on The Jelly Effect I stumbled across a set of videos by Andy Bounds explaining the various concepts. Afters, the one linked to above, was the one that seemed most relevant to me right now. The others can also be found at youtube. These are promotional videos, with the Afters of making us want to buy the book, but there is good substance in there too.
