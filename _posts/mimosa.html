I paid my first ever visit to the Design Museum yesterday, mainly to see the Kenneth Grange - making Britain Modern exhibition which I found fascinating. Whilst there I also popped upstairs to the Brit Insurance Designs of the Year exhibition which featured, amongst other things, the beautiful and engaging Mimosa . It is an exhibit that you can interact with, and had character (mainly due to one of the "flowers" having a twitch that made it seem eager to be interacted with). Very satisfying and playful. A thoroughly enjoyable visit, and a lovely space as well.
