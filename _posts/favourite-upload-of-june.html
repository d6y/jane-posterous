Richard and Skitters at Lancing

In June, I uploaded 71 images to flickr. After a bit of consideration, I think this one is my favourite.

It was taken using my new Blackbird, Fly filled with a roll of the lovely (and rapidly becoming my default choice) Kodak Portra 400VC. It was taken at Lancing and features Richard and Skitters.

The photo has had no post processing. The film was processed and scanned by Colourstream in Brighton.
