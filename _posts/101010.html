As I mentioned a month or so ago I planned (and did) take part in the 101010 photography project.

As I mention in the explanation of the photo:
I originally wanted to do something relevant to the day here in Sussex. I tried to take photos of the turning colours of leaves against the glorious blue sky, but I didn't really like any of them. So instead I went with a view upwards through a stave of power cables and into the deep blue beyond spotted whilst out walking the countryside with husband and dog.

This photograph has been included in two 101010 galleries, iPhonetastic and colour theory. Flickr have also recently highlighted lots of other 101010 photographs in a series of 101010 related blog posts.  There are lots of 101010 tagged photos, so go take a look.
