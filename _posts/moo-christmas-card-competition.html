title: Moo Christmas Card competition date: 2007/10/28 14:56:00 -0700 tags: [{"name":"photography","id":3774214}] author: Jane Dallaway alias: /moo-christmas-card-competition The lovely people at Moo have launched a competition which involves getting your images printed on charity Christmas Cards.
