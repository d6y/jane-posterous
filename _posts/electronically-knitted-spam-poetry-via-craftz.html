Artist duo Mar Canet and Varvara Guljajeva like to bring digital culture and traditional crafting together. They've made a neat new work called "SPAMpoetry," in which they used a hacked knitting machine to knit a series of poems made up of content from spam emails.

via blog.craftzine.com

Something about this project appeals to me. It seems like such a lot of work to produce 3D wooly spam.
