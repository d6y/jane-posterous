title: Lightroom as default photo application date: 2007/03/16 11:56:00 -0700 tags: [{"name":"photography","id":3774348},{"name":"tool","id":58668522}] author: Jane Dallaway alias: /lightroom-as-default-photo-application My copy of Lightroom arrived today, so I've installed it and its all happy and lovely except I couldn't work out how to get it to be the default image application when I plug either the camera or a card reader in. As usual with all things Mac, Richard came to the rescue and found a clue in the iPhoto help. So, I simply opened Image Capture, selected Preferences and set the "When a camera is connected open in" option to be Adobe Lightroom. I've just plugged the camera in to check and its all working well.
