One of the TED talks that were shown in video form at TEDxBrighton last week was Jill Bolte Taylor's stroke of insight.  The video failed to play through to completion, stalling on a couple of occasions.  Each time it resumed I was instantly re-engaged and drawn into the story. I watched it in full at home over the weekend, and again thought it was incredible - its informative, engaging and emotional.  I attempted to describe it to a friend last night, and figured, the only way was to send her the link, which I did. It made me realise I should share it more, so, if you haven't already watched it, I recommend spending 20 minutes watching it.
