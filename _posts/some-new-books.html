I headed over to the Borders closing down sale the other day and picked up a few more photography books (as if my shelf wasn't already creaking!). The new additions were:

  • The Hot Shoe Diaries: Big light from small flashes by Joe McNally - bought to learn more about flash lighting and off-camera flash after attending my recent course
  • The Digital Photography Book Volume 3 by Scott Kelby - I already have volumes 1 and 2, so seemed foolish not to complete the set
  • Penguin by Frans Lanting - I love penguins, a photo book of penguins for under £3 seemed like something I shouldn't miss


So, yet more books to read... I love books, but I never seem to get the opportunity to study them as much as I'd like. Maybe another thing for the 2010 to do list?
