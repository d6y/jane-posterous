We visited the Anchor Inn in Barcombe the other week, and whilst there I took a couple of photos of the boats on the river.

Something about this one really pleases me, I think it is the simplicity and amount of negative space. Oh, and I love the colours of it.

I've spent an hour or so this evening updating this website to have a black background rather than white - initially just for the photography section, but now across all pages (I hope!) - primarily for showing off photographs - both mine and belong to other people - in a much better manner.
