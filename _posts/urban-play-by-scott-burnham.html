Scott Burhnam was one of the speakers at The Story on Friday. As with many of the speakers, I'd not seen any of his work before, and found the whole Urban Play project rather wonderful. But, especially this part of the project - 250,000 euro cent pieces used to spell out "obsessions make my life worse and my work better" all done with the knowledge that it would be "perfect" for only a short while before the coins got rearranged/redistributed. Lovely!
