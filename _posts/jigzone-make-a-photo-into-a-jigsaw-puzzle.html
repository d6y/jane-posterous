title: JigZone - make a photo into a jigsaw puzzle date: 2009/01/07 05:08:00 -0800 tags: [{"name":"photography","id":3774101},{"name":"review","id":3772129},{"name":"tool","id":3772128}] author: Jane Dallaway alias: /jigzone-make-a-photo-into-a-jigsaw-puzzle I found a link to jigzone over the holidays and decided to give it a go today. So, I took one of my Lego Santa photos, uploaded it to their server and generated a 48 piece classic jigsaw. There are quite a lot of different options, like 240 piece classic and 91 piece lizards but I figured I'd stick with a simple-ish 48 pieces.

This isn't something I can see me doing very often, but it was a fun way to spend a few minutes
