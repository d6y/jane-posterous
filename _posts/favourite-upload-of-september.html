Whiteford Sands
Originally uploaded by Jane Dallaway

In September I uploaded 36 public photos to flickr.

This photo is of Whiteford Sands on the Gower Peninsular in Wales.

We walked through a Nature Reserve to get to the sand dunes - so lovely big trees in amongst the sands - and then popped out onto a beach to see this. The Whiteford Lighthouse can just be seen in the background. Beautiful, beautiful place - and we had it pretty much to ourselves.

It was taken with my Mum's old Balda Baldixette using Kodak Portra 400VC

As with the rest of my photos taken using film, the photo has had no post processing and was developed and scanned by Colourstream in Brighton.
